NounsDAO Alt Front-End Explorer ⌐◨-◨

A running list of active NounsDAO front-ends.

Nouns.wtf

The original NounsDAO front end. Developed by the Nounders. Auctions, voting, NounsDAO resources, links and more.

Voter.wtf

Voter reason streams for each prop sortable by time submitted or split by For vs Against votes.

Retro funded via proposal 314: 10e

MMMOGU.com

Searchable rss style feed for Nouns voting and bidding. Board that sorts by proposals and allows you to see who votes For or Against. It also shows who has not voted yet alongside which way they typically lean, For or Against, on proposals.

Retro funded 15eth via Proposal #321.

NounsAgora.com/

Nouns voters profiles with delegation statements. On-chain and PropHouse voting records. Ability to auction and bid on votes.

Funded via PropHouse Round: Nouns Governance Clients and Proposal 154. Total funding: 240e

Federation.wtf

Nouns Governance Pools: Bid on pooled Nouns token votes per proposal. Other various governance tools.

Funded via NounsDAO proposal 229. Lil nouns proposal 69, and PropHouse Mandated Governance Round. Total funding: 257e

HouseOfNouns.wtf

Sortable stream of On-chain Proposals with ability to comment. Ability to switch between NounsDAO and other Nounish DAOs.

Funded via proposal 292 and PropHouse: Nouns Governance Clients for a total of 115e

NounOClock.app/

Bid on the current NounsDAO auction. Count down until the next auction. Live chat for the Nounish community and links to the daily Noun Square Twitter Space.

Funded 25e via PropHouse: Nouns Clients Proof of Concepts

Shenounigans.live

Daily auctions with live feed of on-chain actions via the “auctioneer,” with a live chat. Traits are displayed alongside each Noun.

Funded 25e via PropHouse: Nouns Clients Proof of Concepts

Pronouns.gg

View or bid on current auction. Breaks down traits, and trait floor for each Noun. Also displays book value and %-change from previous auction.

Funded 25e via PropHouse: Nouns Clients Proof of Concepts

NounsPoap.com

Daily auctions with claimable Poap for the top 5 bidders.

Funded via proposal 93 for 88e.

Nouns.sh

Data packed Nouns client.

Funded 25e via PropHouse: Nouns Clients Proof of Concepts
